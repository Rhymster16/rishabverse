# User Journey

## Entry

The visitor enters RishabVerse.

A cinematic loading sequence begins.

The system initializes the digital universe.

A voice welcomes the visitor.

Mission Control becomes available.

---

## Exploration

The visitor chooses their own path.

Possible paths include

- Journey
- Projects
- Innovation Lab
- Startup
- Emotion Archive
- Research
- Team
- Contact

---

## Discovery

Every interaction teaches something.

Visitors discover

- Projects
- Failures
- Lessons
- Ideas
- Research
- Experiences

instead of simply reading them.

---

## AI Interaction

Visitors can talk to RV-AI.

RV-AI knows

- Projects

- Blogs

- Poems

- Research

- Timeline

- Achievements

- Future Vision

---

## Exit

The visitor leaves with

- Inspiration

- Curiosity

- Trust

- A memorable experience
