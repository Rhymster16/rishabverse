Version: 1.0

# Vision

RishabVerse is not a portfolio.

It is a living digital ecosystem.

The objective is not to showcase projects.

The objective is to let visitors experience the evolution of one human being through engineering, creativity, research, leadership and continuous learning.

Every interaction should feel intentional.

Visitors should remember the experience more than the technology.
